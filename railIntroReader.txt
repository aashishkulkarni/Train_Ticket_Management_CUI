Welcome to RailNetwork NZ! 
--------------------------- 

-> We ensure you travel safely across New Zealand in luxury trains. 

-> Please note that you can only travel from cities in the selected train express only.
   
-> You cannot book a ticket to go from a city mentioned in Northern Express to a city mentioned in Southern Express or vice versa.

-> NOTE: You cannot book a ticket to travel from North Island to South Island or vice versa. 
  (Eg. passengers cannot book ticket from Auckland to Queenstown or Napier to Nelson etc.)

We have 2 train networks-
 
Northern Express: Auckland, Wellington, Tauranga, Hamilton, Napier, Whangerai 
Southern Express: Christchurch, Queenstown, Dunedin, Nelson, Invercargill
